Troubleshooting


TROUBLESHOOTING

* Check your internet connection, make sure your smart device is connected to the internet.Specifically for IOS devices:

1. Download the latest version of our app. (Latest should be version 1. 8).
2. Click on the SETTINGS icon from the home page of Apple device.
3. Click on "GENERAL" tab.
4. Click on "Reset" (on General tab this button will be all the way at the bottom).
5. Click on "Reset All Settings".
